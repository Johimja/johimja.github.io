About Johimja

Johimja is a Norwegian Alternative Metal / Progressive Rock project by Atle Stray. Blending heavy riffs, atmospheric textures, and intricate compositions, Johimja creates immersive sonic experiences that explore themes of inner conflict, longing, and existential discovery.

Founded in 2024, Johimja has released the album "Discordant Memories" and multiple singles including "The Ferryman," "Temple of None," and "Segmental Noxious."
